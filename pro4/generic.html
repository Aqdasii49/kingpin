About-us:-

SST Public School Rashidabad (SSTPSR) is a residential school in Sindh, Pakistan, emphasizing holistic education and character development. Established in 2005, it aims to provide quality education to students from diverse backgrounds within a supportive boarding environment. The school focuses on nurturing responsible citizens and leaders by offering a comprehensive curriculum and a wide range of extracurricular activities. SSTPSR is part of the larger Rashidabad project, which aims to create model towns with integrated facilities for education, health, and socio-economic development in rural areas

Dear Students, Parents, and Esteemed Members of the SST Community, It is with great honor and enthusiasm that I step into the role of Principal at SST Public School, Rashidabad. Our vision at SST Public School remains steadfast: to inspire our students to 'Aim High' and achieve excellence in all aspects of their lives. We believe in nurturing each student's inherent potential, fostering a spirit of curiosity, critical thinking, and a deep sense of social responsibility. The 'Sargodhian Spirit' is at the heart of our institution. It embodies the principles of effective education, benevolence, and the continuous pursuit of knowledge and personal growth. As we move forward, I am committed to upholding these values and ensuring that our students receive a holistic education that prepares them not only for academic success but also for meaningful contributions to society. Our goals include creating an environment that encourages creativity, the liberation of thought, and the development of human values. We will continue to emphasize national harmony, critical thinking, and leadership skills, guided by our motto: 'Excel to Lead.' I am excited to work with our dedicated staff, supportive parents, and, most importantly, our bright and talented students. Together, we will strive to uphold the highest standards of quality and ensure that every student has the opportunity to succeed, regardless of their background. Thank you for your warm welcome. I look forward to meeting you all and embarking on this journey together.
Hats off to SOBA!
Pakistan Zindabad!
Air Vice Mshl (Retd)
