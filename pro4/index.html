Real Gem:-

Shabeer Ahmed Khan

” Joined the Air Force after M Sc ( Hons). 65 War broke out 2 months down the line. Our instructor Flt Lt Khalid embraced Shahadat on the day of advising us ” LIVE FOR OTHERS”- that shaped our life. We were ourselves combat pilots in 1971 War flying B-57. The events of 71 re-enforced our faith on the purpose of life. Served as Directing Staff Air War College, Commandant PAF Academy, Base Commander PAF Faisal. Retired in 1997. Set out the remaining life in Service to Humanity. With the blessing of Allah SWT & collaboration of PAF colleagues initiated setting a pilot project encompassing all social sectors for the poor rural.Hope and pray that Allah gives us the life enough to see the culmination of the project – for the posterity to emulate in other provinces.”
